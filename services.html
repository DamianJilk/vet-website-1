Wellness Care & Preventative Care

Our Wellness Exams & Preventative Care services are designed to keep your pet healthy and prevent illnesses before they occur. Regular check-ups help us monitor your pet’s overall health and catch potential issues early. Services include comprehensive physical exams, vaccinations, flea and tick prevention, and routine health screenings. We also offer personalized wellness plans tailored to your pet’s age, breed, and health needs.

Emergency & Urgent Care

At Happy Paws Veterinary Clinic, we understand that emergencies can happen at any time. Our Emergency & Urgent Care services provide immediate medical attention for pets experiencing sudden illnesses or injuries. Whether your pet has been in an accident, is showing signs of severe pain, or has developed a sudden health issue, our experienced team is ready to provide prompt and effective care to stabilize and treat your pet.

Dental Care

Maintaining your pet’s dental health is crucial for their overall well-being. Our Dental Care services include professional cleanings, dental exams, and treatments for common dental problems such as periodontal disease, tooth decay, and infections. Regular dental check-ups and cleanings help prevent plaque buildup, maintain healthy gums, and ensure that your pet’s teeth remain strong and free from pain. Our goal is to help your pet maintain a bright and healthy smile.
